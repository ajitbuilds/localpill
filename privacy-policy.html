Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy is published in accordance with the provisions of the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the rules made thereunder, including the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011.

LocalPill is operated by UpcharMitra Healthtech Pvt. Ltd. ("Company", "we", "our", or "us"). This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use the LocalPill mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name and Age: Collected during account setup for personalization.
  • Phone Number: Used for OTP-based authentication.
  • Location Data: Used to find nearby pharmacies and provide directions. Accessed only with your explicit permission.

Sensitive Personal Data:

  • Prescription Images: Uploaded voluntarily when submitting medicine enquiries. Stored securely in Firebase Storage.
  • Health-related Data: Medicine names and enquiry details shared for the purpose of facilitating availability checks.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

  • Account creation and OTP-based authentication.
  • Routing medicine enquiries to nearby pharmacies.
  • Displaying nearby pharmacies on a map with real-time availability.
  • Facilitating communication between patients and pharmacies.
  • Service improvement, analytics, and crash reporting.
  • Legal compliance under applicable Indian laws.

3. Data Storage & Security

We implement Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures as required under the Information Technology Act, 2000. Your data is stored using Google Firebase services (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Realtime Database, Cloud Storage) with encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, database, storage, analytics, crash reporting, and performance monitoring.
  • Google Maps: Map display, geocoding, and directions.
  • Expo: Application framework and OTA updates.

These services may collect usage data in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Your prescription images and enquiry details are shared only with the pharmacies you choose to interact with through the platform.

6. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

8. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for location access at any time via device settings.
  • Request information about how your data is being processed.
